There is something truly special about photographing families in their own spaces — it helps make the shoot so much more personal. Mesi and Brad wanted to capture their beautiful family of three before welcoming their fourth member into the world, and what a setting they had.

Their Bali home was flooded with incredible light, with bright open rooms and a stunning outdoor living area that made every frame feel effortless. Sessions like this are a reminder that the everyday moments — the laughter, the closeness, the quiet joy of family life — are every bit as worth preserving as the big milestones.

If you're travelling or based abroad and would love to document your family in your own space, I would absolutely love to hear from you.
