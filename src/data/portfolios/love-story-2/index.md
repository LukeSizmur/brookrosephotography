There is nothing I love more than photographing awesome couples in new destinations. Demi and Sander joined me for an early sunrise session on the beach in Thailand — and what a backdrop it was. Even in the heat of the morning, it was such fun photographing them together, and the setting was simply incredible.

Relaxed destination couples photoshoots are one of my absolute favourite things to do. There's a freedom to shooting somewhere new — no rushing, no formality, just two people being completely themselves somewhere beautiful.

If you are planning a trip and would love a photoshoot, get in touch. I would love to document your special time away with your loved ones.
